All restarts provide the following:
  Moderately equipped Civilian Shuttle with 25,000 credits

In case of faction restarts:
  +1 rep to regular faction & -0.4 to its guard (if available)
  Parked on the Guard base selling snubcraft with faction ID mounted*
  Corporate restarts are parked on the capital planet of the region

In case of non-faction restarts:
  +0.3 rep to every faction in the region (Civilian & Freelancer restarts)
  Parked at the capital planet of the region with freelancer ID mounted*

All restart player characters provide the restart faction's native outfit and loadout.


* With a few exceptions
